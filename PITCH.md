# MindPath — 60-Second Pitch

## The problem (10 sec)
Over 2 million Indian students preparing for NEET and JEE experience
clinical levels of anxiety — and most suffer in silence because no tool
understands exam culture.

## The solution (10 sec)
MindPath is an AI companion that reads your daily journal, detects your
hidden stress patterns, and gives you coping strategies built for exam
pressure — not generic wellness advice.

## Demo flow (30 sec)
1. Show onboarding: "Hi, I'm a JEE aspirant"
2. Type a stressed journal entry → submit
3. Show AI response: empathetic + specific coping technique tailored to JEE
4. Show mood chart: 7-day trend with pattern insight

## Why it matters (10 sec)
Students won't call a helpline. But they will open an app at 2am when
they're panicking before an exam. This is that app.

## Tech
- Frontend: React + Vite (mobile-first, fully accessible)
- Backend: Node.js + Express + Gemini AI
- Data: 100% local (localStorage) — no accounts, no data collection
- Security: Rate limiting, input validation, CORS, CSP headers
